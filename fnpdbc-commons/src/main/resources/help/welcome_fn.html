Welcome

FNProg2PDA is an add-on tool for the FNProgramvare software to transfer data from the AssetCAT, BookCAT, CATraxx, CATVids, SoftCAT or StampCAT databases to your PDA or other mobile device, xBase database (DBase3, DBase4, DBase5 and FoxPro), Calc or MS-Excel spreadsheet, Text- or XML file. FNProg2PDA offers as well a PDA database cross converter as additional feature. Designed with Java, FNProg2PDA is comprehensive, intuitive, and easy to use.

FNProg2PDA currently supports all AssetCAT, BookCAT, CATraxx-, CATVids-, SoftCAT- and StampCAT versions that were published since (at least) the year 2004.
