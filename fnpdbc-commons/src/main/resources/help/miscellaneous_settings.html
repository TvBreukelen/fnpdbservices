Miscellaneous Settings

Here you can find some special options for BookCAT, CATraxx or CATVids exports

BookCAT Miscellaneous Settings

  • Incl. Author Roles
    • Includes the Author Role (with the exception of "Main Author") in the Author field.
    Example: "Friesner, Esther (Editor)"

  • Use Original Title
    • Forces FNProg2PDA to replace the Title with the Original Title (when found).
    Example: "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" instead of "Haikerens Guide til Galaksen"

  • Incl. Contents' Author
    Includes the Author name in the Contents, when the Book author differs from the Contents Author.

    Example:
    Author Contents
    Brown, Dan
    		 Foreword (Cussler, Clive)
    The Da Vinci Code

  • Incl. Contents' Original Title
    Attaches the Contents' Original Title to the Contents Title (when the both titles are different).
    Example: "De man die de maan verkocht / The Man Who Sold the Moon"

  • Incl. Contents' Item Title
    Includes the Item Title with the Item Number in the Contents field, when different from the Book title

    Example:
    Contents
    1 - Selected Stories Book 1
    A Good Life
    Lady of Steel (Zelazny, Roger)

    2 - Selected Stories Book 2
    De man die de maan verkocht / The Man Who Sold the Moon
  • Incl. Release No.
    • Attaches the release number to the Series field
    Example: "Foundation Trilogy (1)"

CATraxx Miscellaneous Settings

  • Incl. Tracks' Item Title
    Includes the media Item title to the Track field

  • Incl. Tracks' Side
    Includes the A or B side to the media Item title

    Example:
    Tracks
    A - From 1980 to 1985
    Wuthering Heights
    Schools Out

    B - From 1986 to 1989
    Fade To Grey
    Vienna

  • Use Tracks' Length Includes the Track's playing time in the Track field.

    Example:
    Tracks
    A - (00:35) From 1980 to 1985
    (00:20) Wuthering Heights
    (00:15) Schools Out

  • Incl. Tracks' Artist
    Includes the Artist name in the Track field, when the Album artist differs from the Track artist.

    Example:
    Artist Tracks
    Cooper, Alice

    		 A - (00:35) From 1980 to 1985
    (00:20) Wuthering Heights - Bush, Kate
    (00:15) Schools Out

  • Use Tracks' Index Includes the Track number in the Track field.

    Example:
    Tracks
    A - (00:35) From 1980 to 1985
    01. (00:20) Wuthering Heights
    02. (00:15) Schools Out

  • Incl. Arist Roles Includes the Artist participant role to the Artist field.
    Example: "Alice Cooper feat. Kate Bush"

CATVids Miscellaneous Settings (View = Video)

  • Incl. Contents' Side
    Includes the A or B side with their title in the Contents field.

    Example:
    Contents
    A - Monthy Python Live at the Hollywood Bowl (1/2)
    Ministry of Silly Walks
    Albatross
    B - Monthy Python Live at the Hollywood Bowl (2/2)
    The Bruces
    The Idiot Song

  • Use Contents' Length
    Includes the Contents's playing time in the Contents field.

    Example:
    Contents
    A - (00:49) Monthy Python Live at the Hollywood Bowl (1/2)
    (00:20) Ministry of Silly Walks
    (00:29) Albatross

  • Incl. Contents' Index
    Includes the Contents number in the Contents field.

    Example:
    Contents
    A - (00:49) Monthy Python Live at the Hollywood Bowl (1/2)
    01. (00:20) Ministry of Silly Walks
    02. (00:29) Albatross

CATVids Miscellaneous Settings (View = Contents)

  • Incl. Cast Roles
    Includes the cast roles in the Person fields.
    For example: Arnold Schwarzenegger (The Terminator)

  • Incl. Entire Cast
    Includes the entire cast or only the leading roles.
